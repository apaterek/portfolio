Creative Producer · Seasons 1 & 2

Nutella Spread the Happy, Seasons 1 & 2

Image / video: to be added

Proving a brand platform could be carried by real people instead of product.

Nutella had never made original content, and was entering a crowded holiday marketplace against real headwinds in public sentiment. The brief inverted the usual instinct: strip the branding out as far as it could go, and let four short documentaries about ordinary people carry the platform on feeling alone.

The format's discipline was consistency without repetition: four unrelated lives per season, held to a single emotional register, with the product almost entirely absent from frame.

Image / video: to be added

As creative producer across both seasons, I was part of the producing team at Brand Programming Studios, working with the director and creative leads to translate that platform into execution: casting real subjects, protecting the documentary honesty the format depended on, and holding four separate productions to one tone.

Season 1: 18% YoY sales lift · 50M+ views · Ellen Show partnership. Both seasons premiered at Tribeca Film Festival.
