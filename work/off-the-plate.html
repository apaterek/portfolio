Off The Plate Original Format & Brand

Image / video: to be originated

Building a streaming format into a brand that travels off the screen.

Countless baseball fans share a single ambition: visit every park. Off The Plate is an original streaming format built on that ambition: a travel series exploring the culinary and cultural landscape surrounding Major League ballparks, international baseball landmarks, and the communities that feed them. Created independently, developed with MLB's blessing and interest.

The format was designed from the outset to carry more than episodes: a multilingual structure built for international format sales, and a narrative spine that converts directly into itineraries, events, and products.

Image / video: to be originated

As creator, I built the format and the business around it: a premium guided tourism product drawn from episode narratives, a food festival and night market activation strategy anchored to the brand, and publishing and podcast extensions carrying it into consumer products and audio.

The series is the roadmap. The brand is everything the roadmap makes possible.
